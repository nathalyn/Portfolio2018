Basereach

Branding

This past fall I designed the brand identity of Basereach, a company that prints handwritten card in custom, perfectly imitated handwriting. I spoke with the founder Dan about what Basereach does at it's core, which is helping businesses to express gratitude and make personal and meaningful connections with their customers.

This project had a deadline of two days, so I started sketching right away ideas of a representation of an enterprise solution for a personalized touch and friendly engagement.
