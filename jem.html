Juice Earth Mama

Product, Branding

Overview

Juice Earth Mama seeks to provide its customers with holistic and healthy products for people and their families to better their health. Since it's start, the founder noticed that customers don't use the site for purchase, information or help. They often have difficulty picking products right for them. I redesigned their online experience so that they could pull in more orders and make it easy for anyone to start drinking healthy.

My Role

Some of the key things in product design include:

  • UI/UX wireframes and mapping
  • Customer Research
  • Brand Identity
  • Hi-fi mock-ups

Tools Used: Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, Figma, Adobe Experience Design

Goals

  • Increase activity and sales on the website
  • Create a more cohesive visual representation of the brand.
  • Provide guidance for healthy lifestyle

Research

Who's our customer?

I asked Karen about her customer base, about who needed her product the most and who bought it more.After several brief interviews I could divide her customer base into a few main groups:

  • They have families or some kind of obligation
  • They want to make more nutritious choices or start a new diet
  • They don't have a lot of time to do so

Her audience is driven by lifestyle changes, interesting tastes and wellness, so I started to design with those ideals in mind.

Wireframes

The About page was something that I decided to make drastic changes to by shortening the funding story into something easy to process and clear. In most cases, people want to see the product straightaway, so an option on the about page leads to a catalog. Two key pages I included were the Ingredients page and the Plans page. The Plans page would fix the problem of people not knowing what plan to buy. The Ingredients page is more of a resource that is accessible by the navigation bar, as some people may not be too interested in the fine details of whats inside.
