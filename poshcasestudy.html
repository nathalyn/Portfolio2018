POSH

UI/UX, Branding

During freshman year in college, I started freelancing as an editorial makeup artist just to make some extra cash, but after working in the beauty services industry for some time I saw an opportunity to solve a problem that everyday people have when booking a beauty professional. My co-founder and I founded POSH, the easiest and most reliable way for anyone to book a beauty pro.

The Problem

You probably have an idea of what it's like to be a freelancer or work with one. As a beauty freelancer, common challenges include:

  • Getting clients
  • Managing awkward payments
  • Communication

And as a person who pays a freelancer you might:

  • Spend time searching for a good contractor
  • Deal with late arrivals/nonprofessionalism on the job
  • Anxiety about dealing with a stranger

The beauty industry itself is fragmented and extremely personal, so my co-founder and I created POSH as the easiest and most reliable way for anyone to book a beauty professional. When clients go to our web app and fill out their details, POSH sends them a vetted artist custom select for their needs. And when they're done, that's it. They can leave a review.

My Role

As a founder I did a variety of tasks, but some of the key things in product design include:

  • UI/UX wireframes and mapping
  • Customer/Contractor Research
  • Brand Identity
  • Hi-fi mock-ups

Tools Used: Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, Figma, Adobe Experience Design
