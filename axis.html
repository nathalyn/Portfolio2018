Branding

The About page was something that I decided to make drastic changes to by shortening the funding story into something easy to process and clear. In most cases, people want to see the product straightaway, so an option on the about page leads to a catalog. Two key pages I included were the Ingredients page and the Plans page. The Plans page would fix the problem of people not knowing what plan to buy. The Ingredients page is more of a resource that is accessible by the navigation bar, as some people may not be too interested in the fine details of whats inside.
